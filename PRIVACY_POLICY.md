## Information We Collect

### 1. Files and Documents
- **What We Collect:** The App processes PDF files and images that you select from your device.
- **How We Use It:** Files are processed locally on your device for PDF operations (merge, split, compress, convert, protect/unlock).
- **Storage:** Processed files are saved to your device's storage in designated folders.
- **Important:** We do NOT upload, transmit, or store your files on any external servers. All processing happens entirely offline on your device.

### 2. Device Storage Access
- **What We Collect:** We request permission to read and write files to your device storage.
- **Why We Need It:** 
  - To read PDF and image files you want to process
  - To save processed PDF files and converted images
  - To organize files in app-specific folders
- **Scope:** We only access files you explicitly select or files created by the App.

### 3. Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)
- **What Google AdMob Collects:**
  - Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  - IP address
  - Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  - App usage data (ad interactions, impressions)
  - Approximate location (based on IP address)

- **How It's Used:**
  - To display personalized advertisements
  - To measure ad performance
  - To prevent fraud and abuse

- **Third-Party Access:** Google AdMob may share data with advertising partners. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

### 4. App Preferences
- **What We Store:** 
  - Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  - Default save locations
  - App settings and configurations
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
- **Purpose:** To remember your preferences and improve user experience.
